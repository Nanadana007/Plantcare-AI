About Our Technology

Transforming Agriculture with AI

PlantCare AI combines computer vision and transfer learning to assist in fast, interpretable plant disease classification from leaf images.

Our Mission

We aim to support data-driven farming by making disease detection accessible through a lightweight web interface powered by deep learning.

By identifying symptoms early, users can reduce crop loss, improve treatment planning, and maintain healthier plants.

How It Works

1

2

Get Results

Receive class label, confidence score, and practical recommendations.
